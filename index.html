A Mother to the Voiceless

Archana Bhurtel never meant to start a movement; she just couldn't walk past a hungry dog without doing something. What began with a handful of strays near her home has grown into a full-scale, daily effort to feed and care for over 120 stray dogs across Lalitpur.

Every day, these dogs wait on the streets, in alleyways, near temples, outside shops. They wait for Archana.

She makes sure they are fed warm, freshly cooked meals twice a day. She treats their wounds, pays for their vaccinations, and arranges care when they're sick or injured. She funds it all from her own pocket, because in her heart, each one of them matters.

Though she has only one daughter, she is a mother to many.
